Coffee Table

A cherry coffee table built in the arts and crafts style. The top has a breadboard end with corbels to support it. The slats are resawn so the grain is the same on both sides of the coffee table. The shelf has a subtle arch in the edges to match the archs of the aprons. All joint are pegged mortise & tenons.
