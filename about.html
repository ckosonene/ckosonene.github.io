About

Its all in the details.

I enjoy crafting high quality furniture from wood. I love taking a piece of rough lumber found in a barn and turning it into something beautiful. I try to focus on the small details that you cannot get from mass produced furniture. Hand cut dovetails, inlay etc.

The journey is one of the most fun parts of woodworking, whether it's fine tuning a mortise & tenon with a sharp shoulder plane. Or going for a drive in the country to uncover some old rough lumber an an old forgotten tool.
