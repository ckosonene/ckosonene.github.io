CH25 Loung Chair

This is a replica of "Chair 25" by Danish designer Hans Wegnar. I first saw this chair on Caleb James' website and knew I wanted to try building one. It turned out it was a design by Hans Wegnar and this chair is still produced. I found Wegnar's original drawings in a book and with a bit of help from Caleb James' blog I was able to build it. I weaved the Danish cord myself, it took about 8 hours and about 400 metres of cord. The chair is very comfy which makes it a huge success in my eyes. I built this in 2023.
