Change Table

A butternut change table & chest of drawers for my newborn daughter. The drawer sides are cedar and the top is white oak for durability. All drawers are handcut dovetails for longevity. The frame is double mortise and tenon joints that are pegged for strength, and I also think it's a nice detail. The legs have a slight taper to them with small corbels at the top to give it a bit of an arts & crafts feel.
