Veneered Boxes

My second round of veneered boxes. I tried some parquetry and a four way match for these boxes. There is a four way crotch walnut matched box. A maple burl with walnut edging box. Maple and mahogany diamond box. The louis cube box is maple, mahogany and east indian rosewood. I really enjoy making this style of box, they involve a lot of handwork and the possibilities are seemingly endless. These boxes were completed in July 2019.
