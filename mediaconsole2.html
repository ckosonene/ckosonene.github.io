Media Credenza

My big project for 2025, this media credenza is 8 feet wide and the shelf unit is 8' tall. The majority of the credenza is veneered cherry plywood with a solid face frame and solid shelving. The sliding doors are veneered with ziricote, and unfortunately the finish darkened them so much that the beautiful grain is only visible in direct sunlight. Hopefully they lighten over the years.
